# Research Goals: Fairness as a Business Strategy

In modern finance, consumer protection is not just a regulatory obligation, but a strategic advantage. 

A satisfied customer is a loyal customer who returns. Through data analytics, this project aims to demonstrate that financial institutions practicing high ethical standards and transparent communication can build stronger client trust and improve customer retention.

## Key Research Areas
* Analyzing data patterns that lead to customer dissatisfaction.
* Demonstrating the long-term financial benefits of fair treatment.
* Proposing data-driven models for better transparency in banking products.
